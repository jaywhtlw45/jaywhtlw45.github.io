About Me

Hi, I’m a software engineer with a strong foundation in both technology and hands-on work. I completed my B.S. in Computer Science at California State University, Fresno, where I gained experience in machine learning, network systems, cybersecurity, database design, and backend development with Node.js. My technical projects range from optimizing facial recognition models for bias detection to building full-stack applications using Next.js and AWS.

In addition to my technical skills, I bring 8 years of management experience, where I led teams, optimized workflows, and drove business outcomes. I've spent time as a retail manager, machinist, and metal framer, and these roles helped me develop strong problem-solving abilities, leadership skills, and attention to detail.

Outside of work, I enjoy spending time in nature, riding quads and dirtbikes, having bonfires with family, and working with my hands. Whether I'm debugging a backend service or welding a frame together, I love building things that work.
